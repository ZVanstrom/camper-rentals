About Us

Our camper rentals are a family business started by a love for sharing the outdoors. We (Michelle and Eric) started renting campers out of northern Indiana in 2020, until we retired and moved our business down to the Lexington and Red River Gorge area. Our kids, software engineers and rock climbing guides, have taken over most of our operations alongside their partners.

Our Promise We pride ourselves on keeping every rental tidy, timely, and homey. We freely include the comforts of home — from coffee to TV to a bed already made — because we know what it's like to live on the road. We also know that planning is stressful, which is why you'll get your camper exactly when you asked for it in eat-off-the-floor condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do I need my own truck to tow?

No truck needed! We offer full delivery and setup service. A popular option is setup at the Daniel Boone Coffee House RV Parking in Slade — convenient, central, and right in the heart of the Gorge. We also deliver to any campground of your choosing at $5 per mile with a minimum charge of $100, which includes full setup at your destination.

Where can I take the camper?

Long story short, we're flexible. We'll deliver up to 100 miles no problem and we have no issue with you taking our campers around the region. At the end of the day, we're a small family business and we'll do what we can to accommodate your vacation. Tell us about your plans and we'll tell you what we can do. No hidden fees and no wasting your time: our goal is to make it happen or direct you to someone who can.

What's included with the rental?

  • Basic kitchen supplies
  • Bedding & pillows
  • Outdoor chairs
  • Full propane tank
  • Toilet paper
  • Generator & gas can
    (can be pre-filled at cost)
  • First aid kit

Just bring your clothes, food, and sense of adventure!

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made 14+ days before your trip. Cancellations within 14 days receive a 50% refund or full credit toward a future booking.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, we require a $200 refundable security deposit. The deposit is returned after the camper is inspected following your rental. If you cancel more than 14 days prior to your rental start date, the deposit will also be fully refunded.
