About Us

Crag College Camper Rentals was founded with a simple mission: make it easy for adventurers to experience the beauty of Red River Gorge in comfort. We're based in Slade, KY — right at the doorstep of the Gorge — and we know these hills, trails, and swimming holes like the back of our hand.

Our small fleet of three quality campers means we can give each rental the personal attention it deserves. Every camper is thoroughly cleaned, inspected, and stocked before your arrival. We're here to make your trip memorable—from booking to checkout.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Do I need my own truck to tow?

No truck needed! We offer full delivery and setup service. A popular option is setup at the Daniel Boone Coffee House RV Parking in Slade — convenient, central, and right in the heart of the Gorge. We also deliver to any campground of your choosing at $5 per mile with a minimum charge of $100, which includes full setup at your destination.

Where can I take the camper?

Our campers can be taken to any campground within a reasonable distance. Red River Gorge has several excellent campgrounds, and we're happy to recommend our favorites.

What's included with the rental?

  • Basic kitchen supplies
  • Bedding & pillows
  • Outdoor chairs
  • Full propane tank
  • Toilet paper
  • Generator & gas can
    (can be pre-filled at cost)
  • First aid kit

Just bring your clothes, food, and sense of adventure!

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made 14+ days before your trip. Cancellations within 14 days receive a 50% refund or full credit toward a future booking.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, we require a $200 refundable security deposit. The deposit is returned after the camper is inspected following your rental. If you cancel more than 14 days prior to your rental start date, the deposit will also be fully refunded.
