Who We Are

About Us

Our Story

Red River Camper Co. is a family business started by a love for sharing the outdoors. We (Michelle and Eric) started renting campers out of northern Indiana in 2020, until we retired and moved our business down to the Lexington and Red River Gorge area. Our kids, software engineers and rock climbing guides, have taken over most of our operations alongside their partners.

Our Promise

We pride ourselves on keeping every rental tidy, timely, and homey. We freely include the comforts of home — from coffee to TV to a bed already made — because we know what it's like to live on the road. We also know that planning is stressful, which is why you'll get your camper exactly when you asked for it in eat-off-the-floor condition.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

Delivery?

No truck needed! We offer full delivery and setup service. We'll take care of drop-off, leveling, water/propane fill, slide-outs, black-tank dump, and pick-up in your delivery cost. We deliver to a location of your choosing at $2.50 per mile (round-trip) with a minimum charge of $100.

Can I pickup the camper?

Our team is experienced at hauling and we prefer delivery to reduce risk for everyone involved. However, we're flexible. Experienced haulers (looking at you, off-roaders!) will sign an additional towing addendum and pay a $20 additional daily-fee to cover our insurance requirements and can pickup the camper from a pre-arranged location, usually in Stanton, KY.

What's included with the rental?

  • Basic kitchen supplies
  • Bedding & pillows (for all sleeping areas)
  • Outdoor chairs, sidetable, rug
  • Full propane and water tanks
  • Toilet paper
  • Keurig (w/ K-cups)
  • First aid kit

Just bring your clothes, food, and sense of adventure!

What add-ons are available?

  • Generator — $10/day (comes with pre-filled gas can)
  • Starlink — $60/rental
  • Espresso Machine: Breville Barista Express — $10/day (fresh beans included)

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer full refunds for cancellations made 14+ days before your trip. Cancellations within 14 days receive a 50% refund or full credit toward a future booking.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, we require a $250 refundable security deposit. The deposit is returned after the camper is inspected following your rental. If you cancel more than 7 days prior to your rental start date, the deposit will also be fully refunded. Security deposit will be waived if invoice is paid in full >14 days before trip.
